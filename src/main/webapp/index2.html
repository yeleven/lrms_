金融危机爆发后，我国对流动性风险管理日益重视。为加强商业银行流动性风险管理、维护商业银行安全稳健运行，银监会于2009年发布了相关指引，指导与监管商业银行流动性风险管理体系的建设。从商业银行自身角度来看，面对日益复杂的市场环境以及日渐激烈的银行同业竞争，商业银行必须要建立稳健且完善的流动性风险管理体系。因此，研究商业银行流动性风险管理系统建设具有重要的战略意义

After the outbreak of the financial crisis,China puts more and more emphasis on liquidity risk management.In order to strengthen commercial banks' liquidity risk management and maintain a safe and stable operation of commercial banks,China Banking Regulatory Commission(CRBC) issued relevant guidelines to guide and supervise the construction of liquidity risk management system of commercial banks in 2009.From the perspective of commercial banks themselves,in the face of growing complex market environment and increasingly fierce competition in the banking industry, commercial banks should establish a robust and sound liquidity risk management system.The research on the establishment of commercial banks' liquidity risk management system has important strategic significance.
